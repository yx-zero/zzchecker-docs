## for 1.8.9

#### for rise client, look at [here](#for-rise-client)

use this mod: https://github.com/yx-zero/tokenlogin

open multiplayer tab

click on the **login** button on the right top corner

paste your complete mctoken and click login

done!

## for rise client

go to the **Alts** page and click the **Add Account** button on the bottom

click **SessionID/refID** button

paste the token in the box named **Session- / Refresh-Token**

click login

done!

## for 1.21.5

use this mod: https://github.com/yx-zero/tokenlogin-1.21.5

get [fabric laugnage kotlin](https://modrinth.com/mod/fabric-language-kotlin) for 1.21.5
